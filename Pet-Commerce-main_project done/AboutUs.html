About Pets-Vibe

We're on a mission to make pet care simple, enjoyable, and accessible for everyone.

Our Story

Welcome to Pets Vibe, your one-stop destination for compassionate and intelligent pet care. Our platform is designed to make pet parenting easier, more informed, and more connected. Whether you're looking to book vet appointments, shop for quality pet products, detect early signs of illness, or simply understand your pet better, Pets Vibe brings all of it together in one seamless experience. We aim to empower pet owners with smart tools, such as disease detection using AI, mood analysis, and even breed identification — all built with real-world problems in mind.

Pets Vibe is not just a platform — it’s the outcome of our Final Year B.Tech project in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. As a group of tech-driven animal lovers, our mission was to create a meaningful solution that combines innovation with social impact.

From integrating machine learning APIs to building a mobile-responsive UI, every element of Pets Vibe reflects our dedication to both technical excellence and pet welfare. We hope our work not only helps pet owners but also contributes positively to the lives of stray animals through features like NGO calling and affordable treatment suggestions.
